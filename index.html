About Bluebells

Bluebells is located in Park Close, Longford. It is owned and managed by Brenda McGuinness. Safety is a key feature of the nursery. The nursery is spacious and hopes to create a warm and homely atmosphere, which is important for children going to school for the first time. The nursery also has a secure all weather outdoor play which includes an outdoor safety tiled area and garden area where the children can enjoy playing and gardening.

There is a maximum of 22 children per session with 3 adults. All staff hold childcare qualifications and are Garda vetted.

Bluebells aims to provide a method of education which combines a practical approach, based on a carefully planned learning environment, with a philosophy centered on the idea of freedom for the child. We believe children are intrinsically motivated to learn and they absorb knowledge when provided with the right kind of activities at the right stage of their development. Additional activities the children participate in include cooking, Irish, music and movement sessions, yoga. Children have daily access to arts and crafts materials, a computer, as well as the wide range of Montessori materials.

About the Montessori Method

The Montessori system is structured to allow children to develop at their own pace guided by a trained Montessori teacher using specially designed Montessori Materials. Within reason your child can choose work that appeals to his/ her interests. They will be keen to learn as they are doing what they want to do instead of what someone else has told them they must do. The Montessori Method allows children to work individually as well as enjoying the companionship of others in group activities. Because children are working at their own level they do not feel out of their depth. The children gradually increase their skills, which help them feel self-confident and gain a sense of independence.
