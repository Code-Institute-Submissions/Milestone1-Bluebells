About Bluebells

The ethos here at Bluebells is centered on the idea of freedom for the child. We believe children are intrinsically motivated to learn and absorb knowledge when provided with the right kind of activities at the right stage of development.

Bluebells is a long established purpose built montessori in Longford Town. We hope to create a warm and homely atmosphere which is important for children going to school for the first time. We hace a secure all-weather outdoor and garden where children can enjoy playing and gardening.

Additional activities we do at Bluebells include Irish, baking, movement &yoga, as well as a music session with a musican on Fridays!

The Montessori method was devoloped by Dr. Maria Montessori. The Montessori method is structured to allow childen to devolop at there own pace guided by a trained montessori teacher, using specifically designed montessori materials. The child can work indicidually as well as enjoying companionship of others in group activities.
